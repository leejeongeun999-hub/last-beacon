Last Beacon Support

Need help with Last Beacon: Orbit Defense? Check that iOS is up to date, restart the app, and confirm your network connection if an optional ad is unavailable.

For bugs or account-free support, open a request on the Last Beacon support tracker. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version, and the steps that caused the issue. Do not include passwords or private account information.
