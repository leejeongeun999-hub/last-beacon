Last Beacon Privacy Policy

Last Beacon: Orbit Defense stores game progress and settings locally on your device. The developer does not operate an account system or gameplay-data server for this app.

Advertising and consent

The app uses Google Mobile Ads and Google's User Messaging Platform. Depending on your region and consent choices, Google and its partners may process device identifiers, advertising data, diagnostics, and interaction data to provide, measure, and prevent fraud in advertising. A consent or privacy-options form is shown where required. Optional rewarded ads are always initiated by you.

Game Center

If Game Center is available and you submit an endless-mode score, Apple processes the player identity and leaderboard score under Apple's privacy policy. Failed scores may remain in the app's local storage until a later retry.

Your choices

You can change available advertising privacy choices from Settings, disable sound and haptics, or delete the app to remove locally stored progress. Device-level advertising and Game Center controls are available in iOS Settings.

Children

The app is not directed at children under the age required to consent to data processing in their country. The consent SDK is configured to follow applicable regional requirements.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the Last Beacon support tracker.
